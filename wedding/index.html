Our Story

Caleb and Victoria originally met back in high school when they were in the same small group at Nazarene Youth Conference, 2015. However, it was not until college, when a mutual friend reconnected them, that Caleb and Victoria become close friends. After many movie nights, study sessions, and late-night food runs, Caleb and Victoria grew vey close and confessed their feelings for one another. At 2 a.m. on November 5th 2018, after several months of flirting, they finally officially became boyfriend and girlfriend. It has not always been easy, but after months of adventures, long distance, romance, dates, and some kind of magic, Caleb asked Victoria to marry him.

THE WEDDING

Hanover & Gettysburg, PA

Ceremony

The business formal ceremony will be held outdoors at the classroom builing in Codorus State Park at 1:30 in the afternoon on June 20th.

There is plenty of parking just a very short walk from the ceremony location as well restroom facilities on site. For our out of town guests, we will not be reserving a hotel block, though there are many hotels available in the surrounding towns of Hanover, Gettysburg, and York. The closest airport is Baltimore Washington International (BWI).

Reception

A reception will follow the ceremony at the Mason Dixon Disillery. The reception venue is approximately a 30 minute drive from the ceremony, located in Gettysburg.

Dinner will be served buffet style and will include choices of chicken marsala or eggplant parmesean with a variety of sides and appetizers. Entertainment following dinner will include music and a variety of games selected by the bride and groom. Guests are also encouraged to bring some of their favorite games to share as well.

Due to our restrictions with travel to Tennessee and our financial situation as students, we kindly ask that in lieu of a physical wedding gift, you make a monetary contribution to our honeymoon fund.

In order to provide all of our guests the opportunity to celebrate comfortably, we politely request that only those children involved with the ceremony attend the event. We hope you enjoy the night of relaxation and celebration!
