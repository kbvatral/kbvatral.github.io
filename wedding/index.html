Wedding Details

You are Invited!

THE WEDDING

Gettysburg, PA

June 28, 4:00 pm

Ceremony

The business formal ceremony will be held outdoors on the Gettysburg Battlefield in a grassy-wooded area near the NPS Amphitheater at 4 o'clock in the afternoon on June 28th.

There is limited parking available at the ceremony location with more available along the road just a short walk away. Please carpool as much as possible and if you are a more able-bodied guest, please consider parking along the road to reserve spaces for the bridal party and our more handicapped guests.

Reception

A reception will follow the ceremony at the Mason Dixon Disillery at 5 o'clock. The reception venue is only a few minutes drive from the battlefield.

Dinner will be served buffet style and will include choices of chicken marsala or eggplant parmesean with a variety of sides and appitizers. Entertainment following dinner will include music and a variety of games selected by the bride and groom.

In order to provide all of our guests the opportunity to celebrate comfortably, we politely request that only those children involved with the ceremony attend the event. We hope you enjoy the night of relaxation and celebration!
